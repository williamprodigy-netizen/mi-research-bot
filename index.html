1 · What it actually is

A research analyst that never sleeps. It reads every sales call, application, DM, competitor ad, and Reddit thread, finds what repeats, translates it into angles, hooks, and beliefs, places those as testable bets, then watches the results and rewrites its own playbook — so every cycle is a smarter bet than the last.

trader parallel · market data → pattern → trade → PnL → journal → refine · the bot owns the "journal → refine" loop most teams skip

It is NOT a dashboard. Dashboards report numbers. This produces doctrine — reusable rules like "corporate women with failed side hustles respond 2.1x better to identity-liberation than money claims." The hard rule from your doc holds: AI amplifies, it does not decide. A human reads every word.

2 · The intelligence loop

Your master loop — Sense → Make sense → Choose → Ship → Observe → Learn → Encode → Scale — expressed as the bot's seven-stage pipeline. It runs continuously; the heavy synthesis fires on a weekly cadence.

01

Acquire

pull raw signal from every source, automatically
02

Enrich

clean, dedupe, ICP-filter (wrong ICP = poison)
03

Interpret

extract verbatim language, surface + shadow
04

Synthesize

find what repeats across sources → patterns
05

Codify

patterns → angles, hooks, hypotheses, doctrine
06

Deploy

feed briefs; place the bet against the market
07

Measure

ingest performance → update doctrine → loop
Stage 07 feeds back into Stage 01: every result (win or loss) becomes a new signal. The loop is the product — a single pass is just research; the compounding is the moat.

4 · Outputs — what it produces

Every output is an asset that feeds a creative brief. These live in your ICP Brain zone and compound over time.

ICP / Avatar brief
the 18-section Master Thesis: desire, awareness, sophistication, beliefs, objections, proof — run on real data, not guesses.
Language Bank
verbatim customer phrases, tagged surface vs shadow. The exact words for hooks.
Angle Bank (prioritized)
psychological reframes, ranked by frequency × recency × cross-source confirmation.
Hook + Format libraries
proven openers and structural forms, with the data on what landed.
Objection database
every "why I wouldn't buy," ranked by frequency — straight from calls.
Belief ladder
the sequence of beliefs a prospect must adopt before the main claim.
Hypothesis queue
testable bets: "If [persona] sees [angle] in [format], then [metric] moves [target]."
Doctrine ledger
codified rules: WHEN → MESSAGE → RESULT → CONFIDENCE. The compounding asset.

5 · Architecture — the six layers

How it's actually wired. You already own half of this; the bot connects the pieces and adds the synthesis + memory layers.

1 · Ingestion connectors
Pulls from each source on a schedule. Fathom MCP + GHL API are already live. Add Meta Ad Library + Reddit + comment pulls. Each source normalized to one record shape.
2 · Store + memory the corpus
Supabase (or Notion DBs) holding Swipes, Language Bank, Angle Bank, Hypotheses, Doctrine, Tests. This is the institutional memory — the thing that makes it compound instead of reset.
3 · Semantic search recall
Vector index over the whole corpus so the bot can ask "what have we heard before about X?" Your notes flagged QMD MCP for this — escalate to it once grep stops being enough.
4 · Analysis Claude + master prompts
Claude runs your 18-section Master Thesis + pattern-recognition prompts as system prompts. Extracts language, finds repetition, writes hypotheses. Human reads every word before it becomes doctrine.
5 · Orchestration cadence
Cron jobs: continuous ingest, nightly call-scoring, weekly synthesis report. Builds on your existing sales-intel-eod cron pattern.
6 · Output + review surfaces
Writes the ICP brief + cockpits into the ICP Brain vault zone, posts the weekly Shadow Brief, and a human-in-the-loop review surface to approve doctrine before it ships into briefs.

The standard it's built to hit

Most teams build dashboards — they report what happened. You're building toward doctrine — reusable, high-confidence rules that make the next bet smarter. The bot is working when it can produce a line like this on its own, from your real data:

WHEN: corporate women 30–45, failed side hustle  ·  MESSAGE: identity liberation over money claims  ·  RESULT: +40% application rate  ·  CONFIDENCE: 8/10

That sentence is worth more than any dashboard. A year of them is something a competitor cannot copy.
